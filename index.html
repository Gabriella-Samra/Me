Luxtripper Limited - Product Owner and Manager (2013 - 2017)

At Luxtripper I wore many hats and kept up with the demands of a challenging, fast paced startup environment. I employed waterfall, Scrum and Kanban software development methodologies as appropriate for their respective projects. Working closely with the developers, I performed business and data analysis, and trained end-users on the systems’ usage. I used JIRA, Asana and Trello to create and assign stories and deliverables to developers.

A key part of my job was stakeholder management. I was responsible for ensuring that the product roadmap for each project accurately reflected the stakeholders’ concerns, and that they were kept up to date with the statuses of their issues. Furthermore, I was very commonly responsible for sourcing and negotiating third party contracts for the resources required in the development and operation of our projects. As a key member of the leadership team I worked closely with the CEO on funding projects, as well as brand recognition projects by creating presentations, running workshops, as well as hosting numerous events on digital marketing.

I planned and managed the development of a new search engine with the tech team, worked with designers to improve the site’s UX, introduced a review scheme, sourced external content to keep overheads low, and verified the changes via A/B testing and user labs. As the one constant member of the team, I took it upon myself to ensure continuity and a sense of team spirit to ensure that everyone was aligned with the business vision.
